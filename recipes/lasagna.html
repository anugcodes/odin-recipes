Lasagna

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic,crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 half teaspoon salt,devided,or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese,sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

How To Make:

  • Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  • Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  • A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
  • Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
